# Refactor Plan — Hero Section (Next.js)

I want to create a new refactor for the Hero Section focused on improving the visual hierarchy, component isolation, responsiveness, and overall UX/UI consistency.

## 1. The main div

The first div inside the section is the one that wrap everything. This div will be a two columns space, following the hero section design principles: class="max-w-6xl mx-auto px-6 sm:px-8 pt-0 pb-16 w-full"
The left side would be for the LayeredText component only and the right will be for the two lines of text we have right now in the section the buttons will follow a asymetric display.

Important:

Avoid generic AI motion design aesthetics.

Favor simplicity, timing, rhythm, and composition over complexity.

The animation should feel inspired by:

Nike campaign typography / game retro design, luxury fashion motion design, underground music visuals, and editorial kinetic type systems.

Remember to follow the WCAG principles
